Shake Baby Names offers random baby name suggestions at the shake of your phone.Shake Baby Names is an app which offers random baby name suggestions at the shake of your phone. By hitting Menu -> Options, names can be filtered on category, gender, starting letter and name length. Best of all, Shake Baby Names allows you to enter your own last name to see the complete baby name as it would appear on the birth certificate. Check out Shake Baby Names PRO for over 30,000 names!Included categories in this LITE version are Popular names of the 1920's, Popular names of the century, twin boy and twin girl names, hipster names, etc.Other features include a five-star rating system that allows you to organize your favorites, and a history list of the last names you viewed. You can also add your own name ideas to your favorites list.To get started, simply type in the last name (optional) and shake your phone!
